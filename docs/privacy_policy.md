# Privacy Policy for Utilix

**Last updated: February 01, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the Service and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

## Interpretation and Definitions

*   **Application** means the software program provided by the Company downloaded by You on any electronic device, named Utilix.
*   **Country** refers to: Turkey.
*   **Device** means any device that can access the Service such as a computer, a cellphone or a digital tablet.
*   **Personal Data** is any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
*   **Service** refers to the Application.

## Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

### Types of Data Collected

**Utilix does not collect or store any personal data.**

### Camera Permission
Utilix requests permission to access your device's camera for the exclusive purpose of:
1.  Operating the **Flashlight** feature.
2.  Scanning **QR Codes**.
3.  Using the **Magnifier** and **Mirror** tools.

**No image or video data is transmitted, stored, or shared with third parties.** All processing happens locally on your device in real-time.

## Information Retention
Since we do not collect any personal data, we do not retain any data.

## Disclosure of Your Personal Data
We do not sell, trade, or rent Users personal identification information to others.

## Children's Privacy
Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

## Links to Other Websites
Our Service may contain links to other websites that are not operated by Us (e.g., when scanning a QR code). If You click on a third party link, You will be directed to that third party's site. We strongly advise You to review the Privacy Policy of every site You visit.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update Our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify You of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
